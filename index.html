What is TEDxJUET?

TEDxJUET is a local, independently organized event at JUET Guna that strives to recreate the unique experience found at TED, where the world’s leading thinkers and doers congregate to share what they are most passionate about. We aim to provide a platform for curious individuals who seek to discover ideas and spark conversations in their own community. In the past we have successfully brought together people from across the length and breadth of our country along with locals in the past, to not just motivate our students but, also to inspire them to form a positive environment to stimulate the desire to collaborate and change not just the world of technology and innovation, but every field that they touch. It is an event that aims to celebrate and propagate the power of ideas among the denizens of the magnificent campus of JUET.

TEDxJUET

Who will be speaking at TEDxJUET 2019?
Speakers will be announced in the weeks leading up to the event. Keep an eye on our communications and social media posts to receive our speaker announcements!
How are speakers and performers selected?
Nominations are screened and selected by the TEDxJUET team. Considerations include importance and relevancy of topic, skill and presence of speaker or performer, affiliation to JUET, uniqueness and fit with the year’s theme.
How are students involved?
Each year we assemble a small team of JUET students who help with management, curation, campus promotion, marketing, social media and tech support for our presenters.
What is the schedule and timing for TEDxJUET 2019?
TEDxJUET is a full day event, with registration starting at 9AM. The first session will begin at 10:00 sharp, so come early to have time to check-in and explore the Hub before the sessions start! The day includes 2 sessions of talks, with the final session ending at 5:30pm. Immediately following the final session, we hope that you can join us for the Networking Session to connect with other attendees, speakers, team and volunteers.
Can I transfer my registration to someone else?
Registrations for the Event may be transferred if the proposed Transferee is also a Member of the TEDxJUET community, with a completed profile, and the request for transfer is received in writing to us at tedxjuet18@gmail.com prior to 21st Febuary 2019.
I want to buy a number of tickets for my outside friends?
There are no such provision for outsiders as of now except through a corporate partnership which have a wide variety of benefits, including easy registration for groups. But, still you can drop your request at: tedxjuet18@gmail.com
What our price list?
The event is free of cost for JUETians, but you have to register yourself first at our website.
